It's often asked, "what's the best state in the country?"

Some say New York. Some say California. I imagine if you're from Washington you might say Washington, or Ohio if you're from there.

Those are all great states. I have visited them and enjoyed them a lot. But the simple truth is that Maine is definitively the best state in the union. This page will give you some idea of why. Click on some of the menu items to learn about Maine's features.
